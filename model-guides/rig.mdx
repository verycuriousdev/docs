## Best practices

- Text with scale & materials; or 1–4 images
- Export GLB first; FBX/OBJ if needed; include maps
- Animations: base walk/run/idle; compose in app

## Failure modes

- Topology: re‑run with clean topology; reduce complexity
- Texture mismatch: upload additional angles.
